About Us

We are dedicated to connecting farmers with the right resources, empowering them with knowledge, technology, and support to achieve sustainable growth.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support farmers across Maharashtra by making it easy to find trusted agricultural resources nearby. We provide a platform where farmers can discover local stores, markets, and services, and share honest reviews to help each other avoid unreliable sources.

"Helping every farmer find what they need, close to home."

How It Works

Step 1: Browse Resources

Search for nearby agro-stores, markets, and services in Maharashtra without needing to log in.

Step 2: Share Your Experience

Sign in to leave a review and share your experience with a store or service. Help others avoid fake or unreliable sources.

Step 3: Build Trust

Farmers benefit from verified information and honest reviews, creating a trusted community network.

Our Story

What began as a small initiative in Maharashtra to guide local farmers to nearby resources has now grown into a community-driven platform. Our vision is simple – connect farmers with trusted stores and services, while ensuring transparency through reviews and feedback.

Started with just a few farmers
Built a network across Maharashtra
Introduced review system
Now connecting hundreds daily
